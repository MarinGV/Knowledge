# Syntax

The syntax used is: `>`

Example:
```sh
> Paragraph 1

> Paragraph 1
>
>> Paragraph 2
>>> Paragraph 3
>>>> Paragraph 4
>>>>> Paragraph 5
>>>>>> Paragraph 6
>>>>>>> Paragraph 7
```

Output:

> Paragraph 1

> Paragraph 1
>
>> Paragraph 2
>>> Paragraph 3
>>>> Paragraph 4
>>>>> Paragraph 5
>>>>>> Paragraph 6
>>>>>>> Paragraph 7
